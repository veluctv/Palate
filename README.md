# Palate Singapore

The Art of Quantifying Taste
We believe that the true memory of a meal resides in its sensory output rather than just a name on a building. Most platforms treat dining like a directory service but we want to treat it like a diary. Palate is built for the curious eater who wants to log their personal culinary history while discovering the hidden layers of the Singapore food scene through a shared lens of flavor.

The Palate Concept
Instead of simple stars and reviews we focus on the intricate balance of a dish. Every entry in your diary contributes to a broader map of the island. Our intelligence engine breaks down meals into eight core dimensions such as sweetness and umami depth. This allows you to track your own developing palate and find your next favorite meal based on the specific profiles you crave.

A Collaborative Living Map
This is a social space for those who take their eating seriously. You can follow other foodies to see their latest logs and explore what the community is tasting today. By combining the precision of Google Maps with the reasoning of Gemini AI we bridge the gap between subjective feeling and objective data. We invite you to document your journey and help us map the soul of Singaporean food.

THE SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

Universal Search Intelligence
* Results merge three powerful data streams
* Local Community Logs from Firestore
* Real world business data from Google Places
* AI generated discovery for niche or famous stalls

Gemini AI Reasoning Engine
* Powered by the Gemini 3 Flash model
* Analyzes meals to determine the unique flavor profile
* Extracts signature menus for every restaurant found
* Enriches search results with global food intelligence

Google Maps Native Integration
* Secure backend proxy for place data
* Encrypted photo delivery for establishment images
* Real time neighborhood detection across the island

The Palate Metrics
Every dish is analyzed across 8 distinct dimensions:
1. Sweetness
2. Sourness
3. Saltiness
4. Bitterness
5. Umami depth
6. Spice levels
7. Richness
8. Texture complexness

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
* UI Layer: React 19 with Vite
* Styling: Tailwind CSS 4
* Motion: Motion for React 12
* Database: Firebase Firestore
* AI Platform: Google GenAI SDK
* Proxies: Express JS Backend

CORE CONFIGURATION
Ensure the following keys exist in your environment:
* GEMINI_API_KEY
* GOOGLE_MAPS_API_KEY

Ensure you enable these APIs in Google Cloud:
* Places API
* Maps JavaScript API

Palate is now ready to serve the ultimate food discovery experience.
